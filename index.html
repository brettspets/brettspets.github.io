About the Man

Dan Bowman is a creative designer with endeavors in several fields. He makes music using devices such as guitars, bass guitars, drums, computers, tabletops, steering wheels, and keys in his pocket. He is an experimental cook, crafting interesting and sometimes delicious recipes, and on top of that, he brews only the finest meads and beers. He also writes generally any form of comedy and/or sci-fi, and currently writes and lends his voice for a TV-style cartoon in the works. He has experience in worldbuilding and character design, and uses this knowledge to create and program video games and the like. He has a very technical design process, usually applying his knowledge of programming and logic to create anything he needs. He made this website from scratch using HTML, CSS, Javascript and jQuery, and maintains it using the GitHub version control system. He is a passionate troubleshooter and an exceptional problem solver.
