Our Terms of Service

Thank you for choosing Brett's Pets Pet Sitting Service for your pet sitting needs, we appreciate your business and look forward to working with you!

We ask that you make your reservations well in advance, as weekends and holidays fill up quickly. Please provide your pet-sitter with a means of entry into your home and all necessary supplies for your pet's comfort.

This full guide will prepare you for your pet sitter's visitation, and it is recommended that you read and print it out for your reference.

We also have a few forms that we kindly ask you to print, review and sign. Click on the green links to download the forms.

The Policies and Procedures form outlines a few things to keep in mind while employing Brett's Pets Pet Sitting Service.

The Service Contract is an 8-page legal liability contract between the client and Brett's Pets Pet Sitting Service.

The last one is a simple Veterinarian Release form, which authorizes Brett's Pets Pet Sitting Service to visit your veterinarian for your pet(s), if required.

Rates

  • $15 per 30 minute maximum visit
  • $75 for an overnight visit
  • $0.25 for every commute mile greater than 40 miles roundtrip from downtown Raleigh
