Our Terms of Service

If you've chosen Brett's Pets Pet Sitting Service for your pet sitting needs, we thank you very much for your business and look forward to working with you! Now, there are a few steps we have to go over before we begin.

We have a few PDF forms that we kindly ask you to print, review and sign. Click on the green links to download the forms.

One is a Policies and Procedures form, which outlines a few things to keep in mind while employing Brett's Pets Pet Sitting Service.

The next one is the Service Contract, which is an 8-page legal liability contract between the client and Brett's Pets Pet Sitting Service.

The last one is a simple Veterinarian Release form, which authorizes Brett's Pets Pet Sitting Service to visit your veterinarian for your pet(s), if required.
